Siteplan

1. Site Name

Senpai & Son

This name is a play on it being about my son and I with a strong focus on anime and manga culture.

2. Site Purpose

The Anime & Manga Hub provides a digital space for fans to explore top-ranked anime and manga, access engaging blog content, and interact through a comment system. The site serves as a resource for discovering series, learning about anime culture, and fostering community engagement through user-submitted comments.

3. Scenarios

  • What are the top anime or manga series I should watch or read this year? The top-ranked page will provide curated lists of the top 5 anime and manga with descriptions and ratings to guide users.
  • How can I share my thoughts on my favorite anime or manga? The blog page includes a comment form where users can submit their opinions, which are stored and viewable by the community.

4. Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Dark Red (#8B0000) - Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) and buttons to create a bold, anime-inspired aesthetic.
  • Secondary Color: Crimson (#DC143C) - Applied to header backgrounds and button hovers for visual consistency and accents.
  • Background Color: Off-White (#F5F5F5) - Used for the main body background to ensure readability and contrast.
  • Dark Theme Accent: Charcoal (#333333) - Used for dark theme background to enhance accessibility and user preference.

These colors are applied in this site plan document.

5. Typography

  • Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif
  • Secondary Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif

These fonts are used in this document and will be applied across the website.
